Carbon App Calculator

Our application calculates carbon emissions based on a general formula derived from an emission factor that accounts for fossil fuel-based transportation. However, an extra calculation is required to consider energy production emissions. The formula includes a variable that accounts for the number of passengers to reflect the emission factor per person for public transport modes. This accounts for the sharing factor, including the benefits of carpooling.
