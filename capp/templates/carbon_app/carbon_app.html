Carbon App Calculator

Introducing our new carbon app calculator, designed to help individuals make informed decisions about their transportation choices. This app calculates the carbon emissions from various modes of transportation, including cars, buses, trains, airplanes, and cycling, taking into account different types of fuel, distance traveled, and number of passengers. With an intuitive user interface and gamification elements, this app is a valuable tool for those looking to reduce their carbon footprint and make more sustainable transportation choices.
